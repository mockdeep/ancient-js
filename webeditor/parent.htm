Here's how it works

When you click 'Open Editor' you will see a window with three tabs: HTML, CSS and Roark. The HTML and CSS tabs update content as you type it. The moment you make a change it will appear in the main window. Say bye-bye to our lovely homepage!


The Roark tab is an entirely different animal. This is our fledgeling meta-language. Type something like:

body
bk green

and see what happens. You can click on the CSS tab to see it translated into CSS. It's a work in progress, but you can have a look at our current roster of shortcuts here.

Enjoy!!
